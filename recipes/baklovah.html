Chef Kaizy's Wonderful Baklovah

"He's fat and it's his favorite. You'll love it."

Ingredients

Bak

  • 2 cups bak
  • some salt

Lovah

  • 3 spoons lovah

Steps

  2. Bake it good.
  3. *Lucky Duck's tip of the day: Don't burn it!
